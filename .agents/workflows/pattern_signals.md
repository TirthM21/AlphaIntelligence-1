1. Analyze chart structure for high-probability geometric setups.
2. Scan for:
   - **Head & Shoulders**: Reversal pattern with three peaks.
   - **Double Top/Bottom**: Testing floors or ceilings twice.
   - **Cup & Handle**: U-shaped consolidation with a minor handle pullback.
   - **Inside Bar**: Contraction pattern where current range is inside previous range.
3. Combine with volume profile analysis for confirmation.
